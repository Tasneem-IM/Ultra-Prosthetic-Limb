Recommended Prosthetic Adjustments

Based on your data, here are the recommended prosthetic adjustments to improve comfort and functionality.

Adjustment 1: Improved Grip Strength

We recommend increasing the grip strength for better control and comfort while handling objects.

Adjustment 2: Enhanced Mobility Range

For improved leg movement, adjusting the range of motion will provide better flexibility and agility.

Adjustment 3: Lighter Weight Materials

Switching to lighter materials will make the prosthetic more comfortable to wear for long periods of time.

Adjustment 4: Temperature Control Features

Adding temperature regulation features will make the prosthetic more comfortable, especially in extreme weather conditions.
